Detect the BPM of your mic input!

BPM of Incoming Audio:

Instructions:

  1. Connect a microphone to your device.
  2. Accept the access prompt and play any audio into the mic
  3. The beats per minute of the incoming audio will be shown below, updated in real-time!


Notes:

  • You can also change your input device to an audio interface on the top right of the address bar for direct monitoring of audio.
  • For accurate BPM detection the volume needs to be sufficiently detectable and the source audio needs a steady beat, therefore this tool is best suited for dance tracks.
  • By default, the detectable range is set to 110-140 BPM only.
  • There may be a delay until the BPM stabilizes. I am still working on making it respond quicker.

This tool makes use of the beatdetektor algorithm by cjcliffe.
